Ducati India launches all new Hypermotard 950 bikes at starting price of Rs 12.9lakh

Italian superbike maker Ducati on Wednesday launched its all-new Hypermotard 950 range of motorcycles in India with prices starting at Rs 12.99 lakh (ex-showshowroom across India). The range will be available in two variants, Hypermotard 950 RVE priced at Rs 12.99 lakh and the Hypermotard 950 SP priced at Rs 16.24 lakh, Ducati India said in a statement..

In The Hypermotard 950 is powered by a twin cylinder engine with power output off 114hp at 9,000rpm and has a 14.5 liter fuel tank

After witnessing the on going success of the all-new Hypermotard 950 in the global market, we're ectremely exicted to bring the hypermotard 950 range in India in two unique flavours--the hypermotard 950 RVE and hypermotard 950SP", Ducati India Managing Director Bipul Chandra Said.

The Hypermotard 950 is a fun-bike an is designed to guarantee a high level of thrill and controll while riding, The model has two riding modes--Sports ofr tackTwisting Roads and Touring for inner cities hops or out-of-town runs or simply for those who prefer a clean,fluid riding style, the company added.
